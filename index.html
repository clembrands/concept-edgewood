What changed, and why

Per your notes, the refined direction takes Starting Point as the base — clean, sophisticated, medical, with the typography you preferred — and adopts the layout principle that smaller sections (more visible at once) read better. The section names and a handful of layout patterns from Modern Trust are grafted in: “In their own words,” “What we are known for,” a horizontal stats bar, and the insurances-with-verify-card pattern. We dialed back Modern Trust’s complexity — no green hero panel, no heavy color-blocking — to keep the editorial restraint of Starting Point intact.
